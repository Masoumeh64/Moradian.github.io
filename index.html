I am a faculty member of School of Computer Engineering, K.N.Toosi University of Technology. I received my Ph.D., M.Sc., and B.Sc. degrees all from the Department of Electrical Engineering of Sharif University of Technology in 2016, 2010, and 2007, respectively. Before joining K.N.Toosi university, I was a postdoc at the Scool of Computer Science at Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences from 2017 to 2020, and a senior postdoc researcher from 2020 to 2022.

My main field of research is the optimization and analysis of emerging data networks. I am interested in various topics in this field including IoT networks, status-updating networks, Green communication-based networks, content-centric networks, and complex networks.
